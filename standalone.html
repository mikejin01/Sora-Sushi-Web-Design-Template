The Gallery

Japanese Modern Classics with a New York Point of View

SORA is a modern Japanese restaurant built on a simple belief — that tradition and invention belong at the same table. Beneath Lower Manhattan's glow, our chefs translate centuries of technique into a menu made for sharing: robata and yakitori, temaki cut to order, snow-aged wagyu, and a cellar of rare sake. Ingredients travel from the Greenmarket and Tokyo's Toyosu Market alike. The room hums; the night runs long.
